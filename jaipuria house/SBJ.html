About Us

Welcome to Shri Balaji Tiles and Marble Home

We are Aligarh's premier destination for exclusive tiles, marbles, granites, and bathroom fittings. With years of experience in the industry, we bring you the finest collection of branded products to enhance your living spaces.

At Shri Balaji Tiles, we believe in providing quality products along with excellent customer service. Our team of experts will help you select the perfect materials for your home or project, ensuring customer satisfaction at every step.

We also specialize in manufacturing modern kitchens and wardrobes that blend functionality with aesthetic appeal, making your living spaces both beautiful and practical.
